Barbequed Pork Ribs

Description

Make a delicious rack of ribs, perfect for the whole family on a perfect summer night! Easy to make, just need a grill, ribs, and lots of seasoning!

Ingredients
  • 10 pounds of ribs
  • Your favorite barbeque sauce
  • 20 tablespoons salt
  • 15 tablespoons pepper
  • 15 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 15 tablespoons onion powder
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • 5 tablespoons brown sugar
Steps
  1. Cover the ribs in all the seasonings, the more the better!
  2. Place the butter on top of the ribs and cover in barbeque sauce
  3. Wrap the ribs in aluminum foil, leave the top uncovered!
  4. Cook the ribs on a grill at 400 degrees until you see the top sugar starting to burn
