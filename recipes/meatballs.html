Meatballs

Delicious, Authentic Meatballs

Description:

Using a mix of beef and pork, along with milk and breadcrumbs, make some delicious, authentic Italian meatballs! Best to eat with pasta or garlic bread.

Ingredients
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup italian breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 4 cloves fresh garlic
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Mix everything together in a large bowl
  3. Shape the mixture into small clumps, half the size of palm
  4. Place each meatball onto a pan coated with olive oil / nonstick
  5. Cook for 16 minutes or until done
